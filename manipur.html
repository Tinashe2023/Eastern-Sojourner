Getting There & Around

By Air

Imphal International Airport (IMF) connects to Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati and more.

Airport to city: ~30-40 minutes

By Train

Rail connectivity to Imphal is under development; nearest major railhead is Dimapur/Silchar.

From Dimapur: ~7-8 hours by road

By Road

National Highways connect Imphal with Guwahati, Dimapur and Kohima. Mountain roads—plan buffer time.

NH 2 and NH 37 corridors

Essential Travel Tips

Inner Line Permit (ILP)

Mandatory for Indian citizens (non-residents) visiting Manipur

  • Available at Imphal Airport and designated offices
  • Carry valid ID and passport-size photos
  • Foreigners: register with FRRO within 24 hours
  • Validity varies; extensions possible in Imphal

Health & Safety

Standard precautions for hill travel

  • Motion sickness meds for winding roads
  • Drink bottled/boiled water only
  • Medical facilities limited outside Imphal
  • Travel insurance recommended

Best Practices

Make your trip smoother

  • Book accommodations early (festival periods are busy)
  • Start early for Loktak/Khongjom day trips
  • Dress modestly at temples and memorials
  • Carry cash; ATMs sparse outside city

Connectivity

Network may be patchy on highways and around the lake

  • Jio, Airtel best in Imphal
  • Download offline maps
  • Inform family before remote drives
  • WiFi can be slow in rural homestays

Must-Visit Attractions

Loktak Lake & Keibul Lamjao

South Asia’s iconic lake with floating phumdis; the world’s only floating national park shelters the endangered Sangai deer.

Lake Wildlife Photography
Moirang area, Bishnupur district

Kangla Fort

Historic seat of Manipur’s erstwhile kingdom along the Imphal River; museums, temples and royal relics.

Heritage Museum City
Imphal City

Khongjom War Memorial

A poignant memorial commemorating the Anglo-Manipur War of 1891; hilltop views and landscaped grounds.

Historical Memorial Views
Thoubal district

Shirui Hills (Ukhrul)

Rolling hills famed for the rare Shirui Lily (seasonal). Panoramas, village life and cool climate.

Nature Trekking Flora
Ukhrul district

Suggested Itineraries

3-4 Days Imphal & Loktak

Great for first-time visitors focusing on city heritage and the lake.

Day 1

Arrival & Imphal Heritage

  • Arrive, hotel check-in, lunch
  • Kangla Fort complex, museums
  • Evening walk: Ima Keithel market
Day 2

Loktak & Keibul Lamjao

  • Boat ride among phumdis
  • Visit Keibul Lamjao NP (Sangai habitat)
  • Lakeside lunch and sunset views
Day 3

Ukhrul or Khongjom

  • Option A: Shirui Hills day trip
  • Option B: Khongjom War Memorial
  • Evening shopping at Paona Bazaar
Day 4

Departure

  • Optional: Shree Govindajee Temple
  • Drive to airport and depart
