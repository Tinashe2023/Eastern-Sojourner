About Our Journey

The name Eastern Sojourner captures our core belief: travel should be an immersive experience, not a checklist. Our journey started with a simple goal: to create a reliable resource for discovering the hidden treasures of Northeast India without breaking the bank.

We do the deep research—finding the charming homestays, the thrilling adventures, and the practical tips—so you can focus on making memories. This platform is for the adventurers, the dreamers, and the curious souls. We're just getting started, and this journey is one we're excited to take together with you.
